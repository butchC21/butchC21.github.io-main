White Space

Unsplash

unsplash.com

Unsplash used a beautiful and functional design, and it is the result of thoughtful and intentional use of white space. The space between each images creates distinction and is separated with just enough space to indicate a relationship with the text and images above and below.

Repetition

Generali

generali.com

Repetition is very imperative in the design of Generali website. They reused the same or similar color (red) throughout the site. I can say that this is a good design practice because it seeks to repeat some aspects of a design, creates a sense of unity, and consistency all throughout. Ultimate, they made a lasting impression.
